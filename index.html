Introducing Patrol Bot—an advanced robotic system designed to autonomously patrol your home, offering round-the-clock security and real-time monitoring for maximum protection.

    Patrol Bot moves seamlessly around your home, navigating through rooms, hallways, and entryways, ensuring no area is left unchecked.

    Stay connected with live updates and alerts through the intuitive Spike Prime and webcam app, so you’re always in control—whether you’re at home or on the go.

    Equipped with cutting-edge motion detection and ultrasonic sensors, Patrol Bot is capable of identifying potential threats before anything occurs.
